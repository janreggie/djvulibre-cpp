DjVuFileCache.h

Files #"DjVuFileCache.h"# and #"DjVuFileCache.cpp"# implement a simple caching mechanism for keeping a given number of \Ref{DjVuFile} instances alive. The cache estimates the size of its elements and gets rid of the oldest ones when necessary.

See \Ref{DjVuFileCache} for details.

@memo Simple DjVuFile caching class.
