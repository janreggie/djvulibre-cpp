\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Blit data structure. A \mbox{\hyperlink{classJB2Image}{J\+B2\+Image}}\# contains an array of \mbox{\hyperlink{classJB2Blit}{J\+B2\+Blit}}\# data structures. Each array entry instructs the decoder to render a particular shape at a particular location. Members \mbox{\hyperlink{classJB2Blit_adddd8e6119e02d0fb69f9b6bcfb66802}{left}}\# and \mbox{\hyperlink{classJB2Blit_a082a0c6f468626340a7cdd52f19cdbf1}{bottom}}\# specify the coordinates of the bottom left corner of the shape bitmap. All coordinates are relative to the bottom left corner of the image. Member \mbox{\hyperlink{classJB2Blit_ae8f46321ff4aaea50b01fc3e42005e69}{shapeno}}\# is the subscript of the shape to be rendered. ~\newline
