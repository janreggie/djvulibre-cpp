DjVuText.h

Files #"DjVuText.h"# and #"DjVuText.cpp"# implement the mechanism for text in DjVuImages.

This file implements annotations understood by the DjVu plugins and encoders.

using: contents of #TXT*# chunks.

Contents of the #FORM:TEXT# should be passed to \Ref{DjVuText::decode}() for parsing, which initializes \Ref{DjVuText::TXT} and fills them with decoded data. @memo Implements support for DjVuImage hidden text.
